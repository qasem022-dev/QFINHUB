A 7/1 ARM makes sense in 2026 if you’re certain you’ll move or refi within 7 years, since rates are roughly 1% lower than fixed loans right now—saving about $150 monthly on a $400k mortgage. After year 7, the rate adjusts annually based on the index plus a margin, typically capping at 2% per year and 5% over the life.
